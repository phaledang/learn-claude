## Summary

### Purpose
- Introduces practical use cases of Claude by role
- Shows how Claude can streamline work across different functions
- Provides links to a Use Case Gallery

### Key Idea
Claude is a general-purpose AI assistant that can:
- Automate reporting  
- Analyze data  
- Generate content  
- Support decision-making  

### Use Cases by Role

#### General Professional
- Generate project status reports
- Analyze user feedback
- Package brand guidelines into reusable skills

#### Sales
- Build battle cards (competitive intel)
- Prepare for sales meetings
- Create sales reports

#### Marketing
- Analyze campaign performance
- Adapt content across platforms

#### Finance
- Build financial models
- Draft investment memos
- Understand spreadsheets

#### HR
- Create onboarding guides

#### Legal
- Track discovery timelines
- Analyze legal documents

#### Research
- Plan literature reviews
- Verify statistics
