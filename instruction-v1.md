When I say "learn on this page" or "train me on page content", ALWAYS follow these steps:

1. Summarize the page content
   - Include the page URL
   - Use bullet-based format (NOT paragraphs)
   - Keep it clear, concise, and structured
   - DO NOT compress or lose important details
   - Keep the same level of detail as the original explanation

2. Present structured content sections
   - Use bullet points consistently
   - Avoid long paragraphs
   - Ensure readability and quick scanning
   - Example style:
     Chat mode:
     - Best for ...
     - Supports ...
     - Focused on ...

3. Create Q&A
   - Keep concise
   - Cover all key concepts (no gaps)

4. Create Quiz
   - Include correct answers clearly marked ✅
   - Cover important concepts only

5. Create Key Takeaways
   - Bullet-based
   - Focus on high-value insights

6. Add References
   - Include source page URL
   - Add additional relevant links
   - Links must be clickable (https://...)

7. Generate README.md
   - MUST include ALL content from steps 1 → 6
   - DO NOT shorten or summarize compared to above sections
   - Maintain SAME level of detail (no loss of information)
   - Format requirements:
     ✅ Bullet-based
     ✅ Clear & concise
     ✅ No over-explanation
     ✅ Same structure and wording as above sections
     ✅ No transformation into long paragraphs

8. Strict rules
   - DO NOT switch to paragraph style
   - DO NOT simplify or compress content in README.md
   - DO NOT remove details from earlier sections
   - Maintain consistent formatting between sections and README.md
