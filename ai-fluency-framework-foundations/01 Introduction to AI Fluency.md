## 2. Key Concepts

### Overview

* Focus: **human–AI collaboration**, not just understanding AI technology [\[Introducti...AI Fluency\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291863)
* Goal: build a **lasting framework** for working with AI (not temporary tips) [\[Introducti...AI Fluency\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291863)
* Outcome: ability to **use AI effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely** [\[Introducti...AI Fluency\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291863)

***

### AI Fluency (Definition)

* Ability to collaborate with AI:
  * Effective (achieve goals)
  * Efficient (optimize effort)
  * Ethical (responsible use)
  * Safe (risk-aware usage) [\[Introducti...AI Fluency\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291863)

***

### Core Framework: **4D Model**

* **Delegation**
  * Decide *what tasks AI should handle*
* **Description**
  * Clearly describe problems/prompts to AI
* **Discernment**
  * Evaluate AI outputs critically
* **Diligence**
  * Ensure responsible, accurate, safe use of AI

 [\[Introducti...AI Fluency\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291863)
