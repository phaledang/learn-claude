## 2. Key Concepts

### Overview

* Final lesson consolidates **AI Fluency Framework (4Ds)**
* Focus: apply competencies across real-world AI usage
* Emphasis: **fluency = continuous practice, not one-time learning** [\[Conclusion\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291908)

***

### The 4 Core Competencies (4Ds)

#### Delegation

* Decide **what tasks AI should handle vs human**
* Human judgment remains foundational
* Ensures effective AI collaboration [\[Conclusion\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291908)

#### Description

* Communicate intent clearly to AI
* Bridge between **human thinking ↔ AI capability**
* Enables accurate outputs [\[Conclusion\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291908)

#### Discernment

* Critically evaluate AI outputs
* Understand limitations and constraints
* Avoid blind trust in AI results [\[Conclusion\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291908)

#### Diligence

* Ensure **responsibility, transparency, accountability**
* Maintain ethical AI usage
* Govern AI-assisted work quality [\[Conclusion\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291908)

***

### Interaction Modes (Cross-cutting)

* 4Ds apply across all AI usage patterns:
  * **Automation** → AI executes tasks
  * **Augmentation** → AI supports humans
  * **Agency** → AI acts semi-independently [\[Conclusion\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291908)

***

### Key Principles

* AI is **powerful but not magical**
* Value emerges from **human + AI collaboration**
* Framework is **future-proof**, evolves with AI systems
* Learning is iterative → improves through real usage [\[Conclusion\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291908)

***

### Human–AI Collaboration

* Best outcomes when:
  * Humans provide **context, judgment, ethics**
  * AI provides **scale, speed, pattern recognition**
* AI should be treated as:
  * **Thinking partner**, not just a tool [\[Conclusion\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291908)

## 3. Q\&A

**Q1. What defines AI fluency?**

* Mastery of 4Ds through continuous practice

**Q2. Why is Delegation important?**

* Determines optimal use of AI vs human expertise

**Q3. What is the risk without Discernment?**

* Blind trust → incorrect or unsafe outputs

**Q4. How is Diligence applied?**

* Enforce ethics, accountability, and transparency

**Q5. Why is AI not a “magic solution”?**

* Effectiveness depends on human guidance and evaluation
