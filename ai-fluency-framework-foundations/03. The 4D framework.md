## 2. Key concepts

### AI Fluency Framework

* Defines how to effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely work with AI [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)
* Built around **4 core competencies (4Ds)**:
  * Delegation
  * Description
  * Discernment
  * Diligence [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

### The 4Ds of AI Fluency

#### 2.1 Delegation

* Decide **what tasks to assign to AI vs. perform manually** [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)
* Focus: task distribution and responsibility boundary
* Questions to consider:
  * Which parts benefit from automation or augmentation?
  * What requires human judgment or expertise?

#### 2.2 Description

* Provide **clear, structured instructions to AI systems** [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)
* Focus: communication quality
* Includes:
  * Context
  * Constraints
  * Expected outcomes

#### 2.3 Discernment

* Evaluate AI outputs **critically and analytically** [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)
* Focus: validation and judgment
* Includes:
  * Accuracy checking
  * Relevance assessment
  * Bias/error detection

#### 2.4 Diligence

* Ensure **responsible, ethical AI usage** [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)
* Focus: accountability and governance
* Includes:
  * Transparency
  * Ethical considerations
  * Risk awareness

***

### Ways of Working with AI

#### Automation

* AI executes predefined tasks based on instructions [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

#### Augmentation

* Human + AI collaborate for thinking and execution [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

#### Agency

* AI operates **independently on behalf of the user** with guided behavior [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

***

### Key Insight

* The 4Ds apply across **all modes (Automation, Augmentation, Agency)**
* Mastering them prepares for **future AI evolution** [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

***

### Practical Application Pattern (4D Loop)

* **Delegation → Description → Discernment → Diligence → iterate**
* Example usage:
  * Define scope
  * Prompt AI
  * Evaluate output
  * Ensure compliance

## 3. Q\&A

**Q1. What is the AI Fluency Framework?**

* A model describing how to effectively work with AI using 4 competencies: Delegation, Description, Discernment, and Diligence. [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

**Q2. Why are the 4Ds important?**

* They ensure AI usage is effective, efficient, ethical, and safe. [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

**Q3. What is Delegation in AI?**

* Deciding which tasks should be handled by AI vs. humans. [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

**Q4. What does Description focus on?**

* Clear communication with AI systems to produce better results. [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

**Q5. What is Discernment used for?**

* Evaluating AI outputs critically for accuracy and usefulness. [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

**Q6. Why is Diligence necessary?**

* To ensure ethical, responsible AI use. [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)

**Q7. What are the 3 ways of engaging with AI?**

* Automation, Augmentation, Agency. [\[The 4D Framework\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291876)
