## 2. Key concepts

### Effective Prompting Techniques (Overview)

* Prompt engineering = designing clear, effective instructions for AI

* Combines:
  * Human communication principles
  * AI-specific interaction techniques [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

* Goal:
  * Improve productivity and collaboration with AI
  * Reduce incorrect / vague outputs [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

### Six foundational prompting techniques

#### 1. Give context

* Provide:
  * What you want
  * Why you want it
  * Background information
* Improves relevance and accuracy [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

#### 2. Show examples

* Demonstrate:
  * Expected format
  * Desired output style
* Helps AI mimic structure precisely [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

#### 3. Specify constraints

* Define:
  * Length
  * Format
  * Boundaries (e.g., no narrative, bullet-only)
* Prevents ambiguity [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

#### 4. Break complex tasks into steps

* Decompose multi-step problems
* Guide reasoning path explicitly
* Improves correctness for complex scenarios [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

#### 5. Ask the AI to think first

* Encourage intermediate reasoning
* Helps produce:
  * More structured outputs
  * Better decision-making steps [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

#### 6. Define role or tone

* Assign AI persona:
  * “You are a Solution Architect”
  * “Explain like a senior reviewer”
* Controls:
  * Depth
  * Style
  * Perspective [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

### Advanced patterns / insights

#### Iterative prompting

* Prompting is **not one-shot**
* Improve results through:
  * refinement
  * feedback loops [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

#### “Secret weapon”

* Ask AI to:
  * improve your prompt
  * suggest better phrasing
* Leverages AI for meta-optimization [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

#### Successful prompt patterns

* Clear task overview
* Explicit constraints
* Structured format instruction
* Relevant background context [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)

***

### Troubleshooting mindset

* If output is poor:
  * Add context
  * Clarify format
  * Break task further
* Prompting = collaborative iteration [\[Effective...techniques\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291895)
