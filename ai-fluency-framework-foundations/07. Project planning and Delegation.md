## 2. Key Concepts

### **Overview**

* Apply **Delegation competency** to a **multi-step project**
* Focus: planning → breaking tasks → deciding *human vs AI responsibilities* [\[Project pl...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291886)

***

### **Step 1: Choose Your Project**

* Criteria:
  * Medium size, multi-step
  * Can complete within \~1 hour
  * Personally meaningful [\[Project pl...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291886)
* Example categories:
  * **Communication**: presentation, blog, proposal
  * **Research**: summarize tech trend, compare products
  * **Creative**: story outline, website concept
  * **Learning**: study plan, tutorial, resources [\[Project pl...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291886)

***

### **Step 2: Project Vision & Goals**

* Collaborate with AI (Claude):
  * Share idea
  * Let AI ask clarifying questions
* Output:
  * Clear definition of **success**
  * Identify **value / meaning** of project [\[Project pl...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291886)

***

### **Step 3: Task Breakdown & Delegation Analysis**

* Identify **major tasks**
* For each task, evaluate:

#### 🔹 Skills & capabilities

* What knowledge/skills are required?
* What AI capabilities are relevant?

#### 🔹 Human vs AI split

* Human strengths:
  * Judgment
  * Creativity
  * Context understanding
* AI strengths:
  * Speed
  * Pattern processing
  * Content generation

#### 🔹 Collaboration points

* Where **human + AI together** adds highest value

* Deliverable:
  * **Project plan with delegation decisions** [\[Project pl...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291886)

***

### **Important Practice Principle**

* Use **real conversation (not one-shot prompts)**:
  * Ask follow-up questions
  * Challenge assumptions
  * Iterate with AI [\[Project pl...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291886)

***

### **Reflection**

* Evaluate:
  * Key insights from planning
  * Hardest delegation challenges
  * Missing skills or knowledge needed [\[Project pl...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291886)

## 3. Q\&A

### Q1. What is the purpose of this lesson?

* Apply delegation to a real multi-step project.

### Q2. Why define project vision first?

* Ensures clear success criteria and meaningful outcomes.

### Q3. What is delegation analysis?

* Deciding which tasks:
  * Human should do
  * AI should handle
  * Or require collaboration

### Q4. Why use iterative discussion with AI?

* Improves quality via clarification and challenge, not static prompts.

### Q5. What is the final deliverable?

* A structured **project plan with delegation decisions**.
