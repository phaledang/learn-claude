### Why AI Fluency Matters

* Enables **productive collaboration with AI systems**
* Reduces risks (misuse, bias, unsafe outputs)
* Improves:
  * Quality of outcomes
  * Efficiency of workflows
* Forms a **core capability in modern digital work environments** [\[Why do we...I Fluency?\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291870)

### Human–AI Interaction Models

#### 1. Automation

* AI executes **specific predefined tasks**
* Interaction style:
  * Instruction → Execution
* Example:
  * Generate report
  * Summarize document
* Human role: task definition

***

#### 2. Augmentation

* AI acts as a **collaborative partner**
* Interaction style:
  * Iterative thinking
  * Co-creation
* Example:
  * Brainstorm ideas
  * Improve code/design
* Human role: guide + refine output

***

#### 3. Agency

* AI operates **independently on behalf of the user**
* Interaction style:
  * Configure → Delegate → Monitor
* Characteristics:
  * Defined goals
  * Pre-set knowledge/behavior
* Example:
  * AI agent executing workflows
* Human role: design + governance

## 3. Q\&A

**Q1. What is AI Fluency?**

* Ability to interact with AI effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely using skills, knowledge, insights, and values.

**Q2. Why is AI Fluency important?**

* AI is rapidly evolving and widely used → requires structured capability to use it correctly and responsibly.

**Q3. What are the three ways humans collaborate with AI?**

* Automation
* Augmentation
* Agency

**Q4. What is the key difference between Augmentation and Agency?**

* Augmentation → human + AI collaborate
* Agency → AI operates independently after configuration
