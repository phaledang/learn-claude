## 2. Key Concepts

### Delegation (Core competency in AI Fluency)

* Focus: decide **what to do yourself vs collaborate vs fully delegate to AI** [\[A closer l...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291883)
* Goal: create **effective human–AI partnership**, not full automation [\[A closer l...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291883)

***

### 2.1 Problem Awareness

* Define:
  * Clear **goal / expected outcome**
  * Scope of work and sub-tasks [\[A closer l...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291883)
* Importance:
  * Avoid delegating unclear or undefined tasks
  * Ensure AI output aligns with intent

***

### 2.2 Platform Awareness

* Define:
  * Understand **capabilities + limitations of AI systems** [\[A closer l...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291883)
* Includes:
  * What AI is good at (e.g., drafting, ideation)
  * What AI is weak at (e.g., deep judgment, context)
* Impact:
  * Select the **right AI tool for the task**

***

### 2.3 Task Delegation

* Define:
  * **Distribute tasks between human and AI strategically** [\[A closer l...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291883)
* Principle:
  * Human → judgment, creativity, decision-making
  * AI → repetitive, structured, or assistive work
* Key idea:
  * Optimize **strengths of both sides**

***

### 2.4 Delegation Strategy Principles

* Not:
  * “automate everything” ❌
* Instead:
  * Design **balanced collaboration model** ✅ [\[A closer l...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291883)
* Requires:
  * Domain expertise (human)
  * AI capability understanding

***

### 2.5 Practical Delegation Workflow

* Step 1: Define vision of task
* Step 2: Break into smaller work units
* Step 3: Identify:
  * Human-required tasks (judgment, expertise)
  * AI-suitable tasks (drafting, structuring) [\[A closer l...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291883)
* Step 4: Iterate collaboratively with AI (conversation, not one-shot prompt)

***

### 2.6 Reflection Pattern

* Evaluate:
  * How AI was used in past projects
  * What could be delegated better
* Identify:
  * Tasks that benefit most from AI collaboration [\[A closer l...Delegation\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291883)
