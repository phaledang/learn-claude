### 2.1 What is Generative AI

* Generates **new content**:
  * Text
  * Images
  * Code [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)

***

### 2.2 Foundation of modern AI (why it works)

* 3 key enablers:
  * **Transformer architecture** (algorithm breakthrough)
  * **Massive training data**
  * **High computational power** [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)

***

### 2.3 Training process

* Two-stage learning:
  * **Pre-training**
    * Learn patterns from billions of examples
  * **Fine-tuning**
    * Learn to follow instructions and be helpful [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)

***

### 2.4 Capabilities of Generative AI

* **Versatility**
  * Works across many tasks without retraining
* **Conversational flow**
  * Maintains context in dialog
* **Task switching**
  * Can move between different domains quickly
* **Tool integration**
  * Can connect with external systems/tools [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)

***

### 2.5 Limitations of Generative AI

* **Knowledge cutoff**
  * No awareness of latest real-time info
* **Hallucination**
  * Produces incorrect or fabricated facts
* **Context window limit**
  * Limited memory of conversation/input
* **Reasoning challenges**
  * Struggles with complex multi-step logic [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)

***

### 2.6 Key principle (Important)

* Best results come from:
  * **Human + AI collaboration**
    * Human provides:
      * Critical thinking
      * Judgment
      * Creativity
      * Ethical oversight [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)

## 3️⃣ Q\&A

**Q1. What makes generative AI different from traditional AI?**

* It creates **new content**, not just analyzes existing data. [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)

**Q2. Why can modern AI handle many tasks?**

* Because of:
  * Large-scale training
  * Generalized transformer-based models [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)

**Q3. What is hallucination?**

* AI generates **incorrect but confident answers**. [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)

**Q4. Why is human involvement still required?**

* AI lacks:
  * Reliable judgment
  * Ethical reasoning
  * Fully accurate knowledge [\[Capabiliti...imitations\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291880)
