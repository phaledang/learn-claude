## 2. Key Concepts

### 2.1 Overview

* **Discernment** = ability to **evaluate AI outputs, processes, and behaviors** thoughtfully [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)
* Acts as the **counterpart of Description**
  * Description → define intent
  * Discernment → judge results [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)
* Forms a **continuous feedback loop** (Description ⇄ Discernment) [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)
* Core goal:
  * Ensure **human judgment guides AI collaboration** [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)

***

### 2.2 Types of Discernment

#### 2.2.1 Product Discernment

* Focus: **AI output quality**
* Evaluation criteria:
  * Accuracy
  * Appropriateness
  * Coherence
  * Relevance [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)

***

#### 2.2.2 Process Discernment

* Focus: **How AI generates output**
* Key checks:
  * Logical reasoning correctness
  * Missing steps / gaps
  * Concept connections
  * Incorrect assumptions [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)

***

#### 2.2.3 Performance Discernment

* Focus: **AI behavior during interaction**
* Evaluation criteria:
  * Responsiveness to instructions
  * Tone and style suitability
  * Use of correct terminology
  * Adaptability to feedback [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)

***

### 2.3 Core Principles

* Discernment evaluates:
  * **What AI produces**
  * **How AI produces it**
  * **How AI behaves** [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)
* Even advanced AI **requires human oversight** [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)
* Stronger **domain knowledge → better discernment ability** [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)

***

### 2.4 Practical Workflow

#### Step-based evaluation loop

1. Request multiple outputs from AI
2. Evaluate using 3 dimensions:
   * Product
   * Process
   * Performance
3. Identify:
   * Best response
   * Weakest response
4. Provide feedback
5. Refine output collaboratively [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)

***

### 2.5 Reflection Signals (Quality Check)

* Indicators that require scrutiny:
  * Possible factual errors
  * Weak reasoning
  * Missing logic steps
  * Misaligned tone or intent
* Dependency:
  * Evaluation quality depends on **user expertise level** [\[A closer l...iscernment\]](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ai-fluency-framework-foundations/291898)
