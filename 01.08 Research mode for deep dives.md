# Research Mode for Deep Dives

Source: https://anthropic.skilljar.com/claude-101/383399

---

## 📌 Summary
Research mode transforms Claude into a systematic research agent that performs multi-step, multi-source investigations. It plans, searches, synthesizes, and produces structured reports with citations.

---

## 🚀 Key Capabilities
- Agentic multi-step research
- Iterative search strategy
- Web + integrations (Google Workspace)
- Extended thinking enabled automatically
- Citation-based outputs for verification

---

## ✅ When to Use
Use Research for:
- Market analysis
- Competitive research
- Multi-source synthesis
- Complex investigations
- Structured reporting

Avoid Research when:
- You need quick facts → use web search
- You need reasoning → use extended thinking
- You need internal knowledge → use enterprise search

---

## ⚙️ How It Works
1. Plan investigation
2. Conduct multiple searches
3. Synthesize findings
4. Provide citations

---

## ⏱ Performance
- Typical: 5–15 minutes
- Complex: up to 45 minutes
