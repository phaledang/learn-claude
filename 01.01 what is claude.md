# What is Claude? — Training Notes

## Source
https://anthropic.skilljar.com/claude-101/383389

## Summary
Claude is an AI assistant designed as a thinking partner. It goes beyond chatbot functionality by supporting writing, coding, research, and complex problem-solving. It is built on principles of being helpful, harmless, and honest.

## Core Capabilities
- Writing and content creation
- Research and analysis
- Coding assistance
- Problem-solving and reasoning
- Learning support

## Key Features
- Large context window (200K+ tokens, up to 1M on advanced plans)
- High steerability (control tone and behavior)
- Multi-platform access (web, mobile, desktop, Slack, Excel, Claude Code)
