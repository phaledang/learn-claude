## 🧠 Summary

Claude is an AI assistant designed to act as a **thinking partner**, not just a chatbot. It helps users with writing, coding, research, and complex problem-solving tasks.

Key highlights:
- Built on principles of **helpful, harmless, and honest** behavior (Constitutional AI)
- Supports advanced tasks beyond Q&A
- Highly **steerable** (tone, style, personality can be controlled)
- Can process **large context (200K–1M tokens)**
- Available across multiple interfaces (web, desktop, mobile, Slack, Excel, Claude Code)

👉 Claude focuses on **collaboration and augmenting human thinking**, not replacing it.

## ❓ Q&A

### Q1: What is Claude?
Claude is an AI assistant designed to act as a thinking partner and support complex tasks.

### Q2: What principles guide Claude?
Helpful, harmless, and honest (Constitutional AI).

### Q3: How is Claude different from a chatbot?
It supports reasoning, collaboration, large context processing, and complex workflows.

### Q4: What can Claude do?
- Writing and content creation  
- Research and analysis  
- Coding assistance  
- Problem-solving and reasoning  
- Learning new concepts  

### Q5: What is steerability?
The ability to control Claude’s tone, behavior, and response style.

### Q6: What is Claude’s context capacity?
- 200K+ tokens  
- Up to 1M tokens on advanced plans  

### Q7: Where can Claude be used?
- Claude.ai (Web)  
- Mobile and Desktop apps  
- Slack  
- Excel integration  
- Claude Code (developer tool)
